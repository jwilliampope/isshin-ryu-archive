Spotlight: Steve Armstrong

Seiunchin Kata of Isshinryu Karate by Steve Armstrong. Published in 1976, this classic work by one of Tatsuo's original American students is a must-have for any student of Isshin-Ryu, and martial arts in general. Armstrong clearly describes the Seiunchin Kata with easy-to-read diagrams and clear directions. This is one of four books on the subject by Armstrong — see more of his works at Amazon.
